# Ode to Code

In silent screens and glowing light,  
The coder works deep through the night.  
With keyboard taps and mouse-click art,  
They stitch a world, they play a part.

A semicolon holds its ground,  
A missing brace — what a profound  
And cryptic error, twist of fate,  
That keeps them debugging till late.

Yet when the build runs clean and true,  
The green check gleams — the joy shines through.  
For every bug is but a clue,  
To shape what's possible and new.

So here's to loops and functions clear,  
To pull requests that bring good cheer.  
In every commit, every line,  
A bit of magic is defined.
